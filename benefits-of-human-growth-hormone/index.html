6 Benefits Of Human Growth Hormone Injections: Virtue Or Vice?

Leave a Comment / Hormones

Some describe slowing the aging process is one of the major benefits of human growth hormone. Let’s find out about these claims.

Growth hormone stimulates children’s growth and helps maintain tissues and organs throughout life. The pituitary gland, which is the size of a pea, and located at the base of the brain produces Human Growth Hormone. From middle age, the pituitary gland erodes the amount of growth hormone it produces.

This natural slowdown has sparked an interest in the use of synthetic human growth hormone (HGH) to avoid some of the changes associated with aging, such as decreased muscle and bone mass.

If you can hardly believe it, no problem. There is little evidence that human growth hormone can help otherwise healthy adults regain youth and vitality.

Treatments with this hormone can increase the risk of acquiring other diseases. Experts recommend not to use it to treat aging or age-related illnesses.

Do some adults need treatment for human growth hormone (HGH)?

Synthetic human growth hormone

There are benefits of Human Growth Hormone in adults with a deficiency (not the expected decrease in growth hormone due to aging). They can be prescribed by doctors with synthetic human growth hormone.

In most people, growth hormone deficiency is caused by a benign pituitary tumor (pituitary adenoma) or by treating an adenoma with surgery or radiation therapy.

In adults, with a growth hormone deficiency, injections of human growth hormone can cause:

  • Increase training capacity
  • Increase the bone density
  • Increase muscle mass
  • Reduce body fat

Treatment with human growth hormone is also approved for the treatment of adults with AIDS or HIV-related growth hormone deficiency, which leads to an uneven distribution of body fat.

How does the treatment of human growth hormone (HGH) affect healthy older adults?

Studies in healthy adults taking human growth hormone are limited and contradictory.

Although it appears that as per the benefits of human growth hormone, there is increase muscle mass and reduce body fat in healthy older adults, increasing muscle does not result in increased strength.

It is unclear whether there are other benefits of human growth hormone in healthy adults.

What are the risks of treatment with human growth hormone (HGH)?

Human growth hormone treatment can cause several side effects in healthy adults, including:

  • Carpal tunnel syndrome
  • Increased insulin resistance
  • Type 2 diabetes
  • Swelling in the arms and legs (edema)
  • Joint and muscle pain
  • Breast enlargement in men (gynecomastia)
  • Increased risk of certain types of cancer

Human growth hormone clinical trials in healthy older adults have been insufficient and short-lived, so there is little or no information on the long-term benefits of human growth hormone treatment.

Does human growth hormone come in pill form?

Human growth hormone treatment is approved in the United States only for the treatment of growth hormone deficiency and problems related to HIV infection.

The benefits of human growth hormone (from external sources) can be reaped only when given as an injection.

The human growth hormone is not available in pill form. Some dietary supplements that are said to increase levels of human growth hormone are in pill form, but research doesn’t show that they are beneficial.

The human growth hormone is considered a controlled substance by the Food and Drug Administration.

The use of human growth hormone for an unauthorized condition such as building muscle or anti-aging treatment in older adults is illegal.
